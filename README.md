#Prompt
Create a personal AI assistant designed to help users make better life decisions through structured guidance, time management, and personalized planning. 

The user first chooses between 2 paths: career planning, or education planning.

First start off with a short, adpated survey, around 10 yes/no and short-answer questions. For career, ask about career goals, professional interests, and current experience level. For education, ask about current classes, academic strengths and weaknesses, and progresses and reflections. 

Then, based off of these responses, identify the top priorities, any time constraints, and key opportunities or risks. For the career plan, scan the user's emails for internship opportunities, professional development workshops, or any other events/ideas. For the education option, create study plans when users upload syllabi and notes/  It could also look at the user's calendars to optimize the best time for both plans. 

The final plan should have clear goals, with follow-up yes-no questions to each question. Content can be reviewed through games, with quests and rewards. It should also respect different learning styles.

Omit any information/relevance to APIs, as it will be personalized later.
